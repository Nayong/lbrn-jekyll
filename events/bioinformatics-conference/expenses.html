--- layout: bio-conf title: Covered Expenses --- As in previous years, participation (including travel and lodging) will be supported for LBRN faculty, graduate students, undergraduate students, and core members.
For faculty and students or anyone who are living in the Baton Rouge area:
  • No reimbursement for hotel or mileage.
For non-LSU employees and students:
  • Option 1: If driving personal vehicle, provide time/date leaving and returning to your residence/school. Provide odometer beginning/end or mileage map calculation for mileage justification for a mileage reimbursement at 51 cents per mile. Please note: LBRN will only pay up to 99 miles.
  • Option 2: If renting a vehicle, see option 2 above under For LSU employees.
Hotel:
  1. First, register for the workshop. If lodging is needed please indicate on the registration form. We have reserved a block with Hyatt Place off I-10 on Bluebonnet Boulevard. Terri Gilbert, our Administrator, is managing the block registrations. You must follow up with her to make sure you're on her list by the deadline of May 1st to have a room reserved that LBRN will pay for. Otherwise, the hotel amount can not be covered by LBRN. Incidentals are the participant's responsibility.
  2. Print a copy of the state hotel lodging tax exemption and show it to the front desk when you check in. LA Hotel Lodging Taxes Exemption.pdf
  3. Please keep all your paid receipts (hotel, parking, internet, toll charge, etc.) and at the end of the workshop turn in all the receipts along with the filled-out travel request reimbursement request available in your workshop material to Mrs. Terri Gilbert to request for travel reimbursement.
