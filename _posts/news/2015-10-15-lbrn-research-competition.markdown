**Objectives**

The purpose of these programs is to to solicit new start-up and shared instrumentation funds as well as for new pilot and full projects for the proposed grant period as well as provide an additional opportunity for junior and senior LBRN PUI faculty to engage in clinical and translational research. The selection and distribution of these projects by the Steering Committee will be based on the quality of the proposals and the needs of the INBRE program.
